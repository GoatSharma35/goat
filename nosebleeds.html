Treating Nosebleeds

Simple steps to stop the bleeding

Step-by-Step Treatment:

  1. Stay calm - Keep the person upright and calm
  2. Lean forward - Tilt head slightly forward to prevent blood from going down throat
  3. Pinch the nostrils - Use thumb and index finger to pinch soft part of nose
  4. Hold for 10-15 minutes - Apply continuous pressure, breathe through mouth
  5. Check if bleeding stopped - Release pressure gently
  6. If still bleeding - Repeat pressure for another 10-15 minutes

After the Bleeding Stops:

  • Don't blow nose - For at least 12 hours
  • Avoid picking - Keep hands away from nose
  • Use saline spray - To keep nasal passages moist
  • Apply petroleum jelly - Small amount inside nostrils

  • Frequent nosebleeds (more than once a week)
  • Blood is coming from only one nostril after head injury
  • Person is taking blood-thinning medication

Prevention Tips:

  • Use humidifier in dry environments
  • Apply thin layer of petroleum jelly inside nostrils
  • Trim fingernails short
  • Treat allergies that cause nasal congestion
