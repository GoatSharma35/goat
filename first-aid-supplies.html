Complete Home First Aid Kit

Essential supplies for emergency preparedness

A well-stocked first aid kit is crucial for handling emergencies at home. Keep your kit in an easily accessible location and check supplies regularly.

ü©π Wound Care Supplies

  • Adhesive bandages (various sizes)
  • Sterile gauze pads (2x2, 3x3, 4x4 inches)
  • Medical tape
  • Elastic bandages (ACE wraps)
  • Triangular bandages
  • Butterfly bandages
  • Non-stick sterile pads
  • Rolled gauze

üß¥ Medications & Ointments

  • Pain relievers (ibuprofen, acetaminophen, aspirin)
  • Antihistamine (Benadryl)
  • Anti-diarrheal medication
  • Hydrocortisone cream (1%)
  • Antibiotic ointment
  • Burn gel or aloe vera
  • Calamine lotion
  • Activated charcoal (for poisoning)

üîß Tools & Equipment

  • Scissors (medical grade)
  • Tweezers
  • Digital thermometer
  • Disposable gloves (latex-free)
  • CPR face mask
  • Instant cold packs
  • Emergency blanket
  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • Safety pins

üßº Cleaning Supplies

  • Antiseptic wipes
  • Alcohol pads
  • Hydrogen peroxide
  • Hand sanitizer
  • Soap (antibacterial)
  • Saline solution (for eye irrigation)

üìã Documentation & Extras

  • Emergency contact numbers
  • First aid manual
  • List of family medications/allergies
  • Pen and paper
  • Emergency cash
  • Prescription medications (as needed)

üìù Important Notes:

  • Check expiration dates every 6 months
  • Keep kit in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Consider multiple kits for home, car, and workplace
  • Customize for your family - include specific medications or supplies for chronic conditions
  • Train family members on kit location and basic first aid
