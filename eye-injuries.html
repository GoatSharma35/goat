Eye Injury First Aid

Protect vision with proper care

For Particles in Eye:

  1. Don't rub the eye - This can cause more damage
  2. Wash hands - Clean thoroughly before touching near eye
  3. Try blinking - Natural tears may flush out particle
  4. Flush with clean water - Use gentle stream from inner corner outward
  5. Pull upper eyelid over lower - This may help dislodge particle
  6. If particle remains - Seek medical attention

For Chemical in Eye:

  1. Flush immediately - Use clean water or saline for 15-20 minutes
  2. Remove contact lenses - If wearing any
  3. Keep eye open - Use fingers to hold eyelids apart while flushing
  4. Seek emergency care - Go to hospital immediately
  5. Bring chemical container - If possible, for identification

For Cuts Around Eye:

  1. Apply gentle pressure - Use clean cloth around (not on) the eye
  2. Don't remove objects - If something is stuck in eye
  3. Cover both eyes - Use bandage to prevent eye movement
  4. Seek immediate medical care - Go to emergency room
