Insect Bite Treatment

Relief for stings and bites

For Most Insect Bites:

  1. Clean the area - Wash with soap and water
  2. Apply cold compress - Use ice wrapped in cloth for 10 minutes
  3. Don't scratch - This can cause infection
  4. Apply anti-itch treatment - Use calamine lotion or antihistamine cream
  5. Take antihistamine - Oral medication can reduce swelling and itching
  6. Monitor for infection - Watch for increasing redness, warmth, or pus

For Bee or Wasp Stings:

  1. Remove stinger quickly - Scrape it out with fingernail or credit card
  2. Don't use tweezers - This can squeeze more venom into skin
  3. Clean with soap and water
  4. Apply cold compress - For 10 minutes at a time
  5. Watch for allergic reaction - See emergency signs below

Home Remedies That Help:

  • Baking soda paste - Mix with water, apply to bite
  • Oatmeal bath - For multiple bites
  • Aloe vera - Natural anti-inflammatory
  • Honey - Has antibacterial properties
  • Tea tree oil - Dilute before applying

  • Check for ticks after being in tall grass or woods
