Treating Burns

Cool, clean, and protect

For Minor Burns (1st & 2nd degree):

  1. Cool the burn - Hold under cool (not cold) running water for 10-20 minutes
  2. Remove jewelry - Take off rings, bracelets before swelling begins
  3. Don't break blisters - If blisters form, don't pop them
  4. Apply moisturizer - Use aloe vera or moisturizer while skin is damp
  5. Cover loosely - Use sterile gauze bandage, don't wrap tightly
  6. Take pain reliever - Ibuprofen or acetaminophen can help with pain
