Treating Cuts

Step-by-step wound care

For Minor Cuts:

  1. Clean your hands - Wash thoroughly with soap and water
  2. Stop the bleeding - Apply gentle pressure with a clean cloth
  3. Clean the wound - Rinse with clean water. Remove debris with tweezers cleaned with alcohol
  4. Apply antibiotic - Use a thin layer of antibiotic ointment if available
  5. Cover the wound - Use a sterile bandage or adhesive bandage
  6. Change the dressing - Keep wound clean and dry, change bandage daily
