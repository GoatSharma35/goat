Treating Sprains

Remember R.I.C.E. treatment

R.I.C.E. Method:

  1. Rest - Avoid activities that cause pain or swelling
  2. Ice - Apply ice pack for 15-20 minutes every 2-3 hours for first 48 hours
  3. Compression - Use elastic bandage to reduce swelling (not too tight)
  4. Elevation - Raise injured area above heart level when possible

Additional Care:

  • Pain relief - Take over-the-counter pain medications as directed
  • Gradual movement - Gently move joint through range of motion as tolerated
  • Heat after 48 hours - Switch to heat therapy after initial swelling goes down
